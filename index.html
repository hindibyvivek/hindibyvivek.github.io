about me

I am currently a postdoctoral fellow working on the synthesis of bio-based food packaging material at the Department of Chemical Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Bombay, India.

My broad research interests are in "CHANGE IT". My current project investigates on bio-based antimicrobial food packaging through the lens of nanotechnology. My dissertation, Synthesis & characterization of bio-nanomaterials for management of early blight disease in tomato was based on development of potent nanoemulsion for fungicidal activity.

I received my PhD in Biological Sciences from CSIR-National Botanical Research Institute (NBRI) in May 2020. Before CSIR-NBRI, I studied at the Barkatullah University and received my MSc. in Microbiology in 2012. I completed my B.Sc. in from the Allahabad University in 2010.
